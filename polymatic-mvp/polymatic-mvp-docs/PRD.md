# PolyMatic Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Vision
To provide a professional OSINT dashboard for analysts, journalists, and geopolitical researchers, combining the capabilities of Palantir Gotham, Google Earth, and Bloomberg Terminal.

## Core Features
- **Global 3D Globe**: Interactive 3D visualization of the Earth.
- **Layer System**: Toggle visibility of different intelligence signals (Aircraft, Ships, Events).
- **Real-time Telemetry**: Live updates of entity positions and statuses.
- **Object Inspector**: Clickable entities to view detailed metadata.
- **Event Feed**: Chronological list of OSINT events.

## Design Language
- **Dark-first interface**: Optimized for low-light environments and long viewing sessions.
- **High information density**: Maximizing data visibility without clutter.
- **Precision typography**: Monospace fonts for data, sans-serif for UI.
- **Tactical telemetry styling**: Neon accents (cyan, amber, red) on dark backgrounds.
